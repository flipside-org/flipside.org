We're hiring a Junior Web Developer

We're looking for a junior web developer with the following profile:

  • good knowledge of PHP, Javascript, HTML and CSS
  • a passion for developing elegant and usable web applications
  • preferably have experience with Drupal
  • organized, communicative and committed to writing quality code
  • a working knowledge of English
  • preferably have experience setting up and managing web servers

Depending on your profile, you'll be involved in most stages of the client projects, from concept development and technical design to deployment. With the right attitude you can have a big impact on the company and our projects.

We are not looking for a senior developer, there is time for exploration and learning in this job, but we do require you to have a pragmatic and hands-on attitude and be passionate about what you do. At Flipside we believe in the power of Open Source and we'd like to work with somebody who also does.

What we offer

  • You'll work for international clients that make a difference in the world
  • A desk in one of the nicest co-working spaces in Lisbon
  • Time set aside to work on non-client projects during office hours
  • Relaxed and informal work environment

We are looking to hire somebody for the Estágios Profissionais program of IEFP. Please check their page to see if you fit the conditions. Depending on your profile and experience, we can talk about ways to complement the package offered under this program.

Apply

To apply, please send your CV and a short motivation letter to hiring@flipside.org before Friday, August 31. A link to your portfolio or examples of your work, either developed in a personal or professional setting, will be highly appreciated.
