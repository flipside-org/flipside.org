We’re happy to announce that, starting today, our team will be joining Development Seed. This is a big move for us, as we’ll be working side by side with a very talented group of people to change the way that data and technology is used to improve societies.

We’ll continue to be engaged with our partners and the community and look forward to do so with a bigger team.
If you’re in Lisbon today, come and celebrate with us at the Happy Hour we’re hosting at Liberdade 229.
