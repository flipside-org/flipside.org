At Flipside we work closely with our clients to create appropriate and usable solutions.

  • MacGyver

    Design + Concept + Development

    Text to Change is a global innovator implementing interactive and incentive based SMS programmes around the world.

    We worked with TTC to develop MacGyver, a practical and flexible system that allows them to coordinate their growing portfolio of activities. Each employee has a personal dashboard providing an overview with the relevant projects, milestones and leads. [A central place to store information on projects, milestones and leads.]

    More information about the system and a demo can be found at: http://macgyver.texttochange.org.
